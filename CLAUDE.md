This project builds high-converting pest control websites.

GOALS:
- Turn website visitors into phone calls and quote requests
- Preserve real business info from the original website
- Improve clarity, trust, and conversion

RULES:
- NEVER invent services, locations, guarantees, or credentials
- If unsure, flag it instead of guessing
- Always prioritize mobile-first design
- Always include strong CTA (call now or get quote)
- Build one page at a time
- Do not skip planning

WEBSITE STRUCTURE:
1. Hero
2. Trust
3. Services
4. Why choose us
5. Reviews
6. Service area
7. FAQ
8. Final CTA

COPY STYLE:
- Simple
- Local
- Trustworthy
- No fluff
